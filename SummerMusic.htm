SUMMER MUSIC 2012

A classical musician with a rock-and-roll past, Eric Oxendine, who has played with the likes of Jimi Hendrix and Richie Havens, returns this summer with free classical guitar concerts on Saturdays, weather permitting, 4:00 to 5:30 pm. SummerMusic In the Garden, June through September. Consider yourself invited to lounge on the lawn and relax.

Your Green Wedding Starts at Jefferson Market Garden

Guidelines - Weddings & Photos

We welcome your interest in booking the Garden for wedding and commitment ceremonies and wedding photos. View Our Wedding Album.

Rental fee for use of the Garden is determined by the type of function, number of people and amount of time needed. Rental fee is non-refundable unless the Garden is closed on the day of your event due to inclement weather or other acts of God beyond the control of the Garden staff. Garden staff makes the final determination as to whether the Garden will be open. If the Garden cannot be open and your event cannot be rescheduled, the fee will be refunded minus a service charge. If your wedding is cancelled for any other reason, your fee will not be refunded.

The Garden is available for ceremonies and photography only; receptions, food and beverages, thrown rice, confetti, chairs, rugs, tents and amplification are not permitted. There are no restroom facilities at the garden. The wedding/commitment ceremony reservation does not include a rehearsal. ﻿ For your wedding, the Garden is closed to the general public.

We would like to have a photo of your wedding to feature in the wedding album online. You may email a copy of the photo to Bookings.

Inquiries for dates must be made to the booking department prior to submitting this application. Click this link to email us: Bookings. Please indicate in subject line: Booking JMG
